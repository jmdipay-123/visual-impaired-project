Object Information

Learn about the objects our system can detect to help you navigate safely and confidently.

People

The camera automatically detects and identifies people in real time, helping you avoid collisions and navigate crowded spaces safely.

Doors

The system recognizes doors and helps indicate their location and orientation, making it easier to find entrances and exits.

Stairs

The camera detects stairs to assist with safe navigation and movement, with audio and vibration warnings for added safety.

How It Works

  • Real-time Camera Detection: Uses your device camera to continuously scan the environment
  • AI-Powered Recognition: Identifies objects with great accuracy
  • Audio Alerts: Announces detected objects in your preferred language (English, Tagalog, or Cebuano)
  • Color-Coded Boxes: Visual indicators show detected objects with color-coded bounding boxes
  • Priority Warnings: Stairs receive highest priority for safety, followed by people and doors
